About Me

I've always loved knowing how things work, and why they work. It's no different with programming. I have an Associate's Degree in Industrial Electronics Technology from Spartanburg Community College. My initial attraction to the field was due to the nature of the problem solving involved in troubleshooting. I decided however that the industrial field wasn't for me and I wanted to be closer to my other passions. When I finally got involved with the idea of programming, I started seeing the similarities in logical problem solving between programming and my previous field of study. Learning more I discovered a ton of benefits to working in the field and got sucked in more and more. Now that I'm doing this course at Thinkful, I'm ecstatic to see all the possibilities I have in my future, and also the security I'll be able to provide myself and my family.

My programming goals are:

  • To provide a better future for my family.
  • To learn new skills that will further my expertise.
  • To write good code that other developers can enjoy working off of.

Outside of programming, I love to play guitar and write progressive instrumental music. I'd love to be able to share it with more people than I already have. I also love playing games with my friends and family, whether it's video games, board games, or RPGs.
